ABOUT ME
    I came to coding after graduating from drama school and wanting a change. I’ve always been driven by creativity, and the relationship between design and development. Throughout my time at General Assembly I have explored multiple areas of full-stack web development, finding a love of CSS and front-end capability in the process. Versed in ‘play’ based thinking and creation at drama school, I’m looking for opportunities to produce thoughtful work and continue to learn.
    It’s unrealistic to believe that 12 weeks is enough to learn every nuance that various programming languages have to offer, but the GA course is about fostering an approach to learning which cultivates problem solving, lateral thinking and a level of engagement which drives the desire to keep learning.
    HERE'S WHAT I'VE LEARNT SO FAR
    Javascript, jQuery, CSS, SASS, HTML, AngularJS, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Ruby, Bootstrap, Photoshop, Sketch, MongoDB, Express, Git, Auth, tokens, AJAX, JSON, Sinatra, Rails, Gulp, Insomnia and Bower.
